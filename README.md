# WatchToHeal: Your Cinema, Your Peace

![WatchToHeal UI Screenshot](https://i.imgur.com/g0P3p3c.png)

## Introduction

WatchToHeal is a sophisticated iOS application that redefines the movie-watching experience. It's more than just a streaming app; it's a companion for cinematic therapy. The app provides a platform for users to discover, track, and discuss films, with a unique focus on the emotional and healing power of cinema. With a sleek, modern interface and a rich feature set, WatchToHeal connects users with a community of film lovers who share a passion for the transformative power of movies.

## Key Features

The application is packed with features designed to provide a seamless and engaging user experience:

*   **Authentication:** Secure and easy sign-up and login using email/password or Google Sign-In, powered by Firebase Authentication.
*   **Dynamic Onboarding:** A personalized onboarding process to tailor the user's experience from the very beginning.
*   **Intuitive Navigation:** A stunning, custom-built "liquid glass" tab bar provides easy access to all the main sections of the app:
    *   **Home:** A personalized dashboard with curated movie lists, trending films, and recommendations.
    *   **Search:** A powerful search engine to discover movies, directors, and more.
    *   **Community:** A social hub where users can participate in movie polls, browse community-curated lists, and engage in discussions.
    *   **Watchlist:** A personal space to keep track of movies to watch, and ones already seen.
    *   **Profile:** A dedicated area for users to manage their account, preferences, and view their activity.
*   **Detailed Movie Information:** Comprehensive movie detail screens with posters, backdrops, synopses, cast and crew information, and trailers.
*   **Social Interaction:**
    *   **Movie Polls:** Participate in and create polls about your favorite films.
    *   **Community Lists:** Discover and contribute to themed movie lists created by the community.
    *   **Commenting:** Share your thoughts and discuss movies with other users.
*   **Admin Functionality:** A dedicated section for administrators to manage content and community features.

## Technical Stack

WatchToHeal is built with a modern and robust technical stack:

*   **Platform:** iOS
*   **UI Framework:** SwiftUI
*   **Architecture:** Model-View-ViewModel (MVVM)
*   **Backend:** Firebase (Firestore for the database, Firebase Authentication for user management)
*   **External APIs:**
    *   **The Movie Database (TMDB):** For comprehensive movie data.
    *   **Trakt.tv:** For tracking watched history and recommendations.
    *   **TasteDive:** For finding similar movies and enhancing recommendations.
*   **Concurrency:** Swift's modern concurrency model (`async/await`) for smooth and responsive performance.

## Unique Selling Points

What makes WatchToHeal stand out?

*   **The "Cinema Therapy" Concept:** The app's core philosophy is that movies can be a powerful tool for emotional healing and self-discovery. This is reflected in the app's design and community features.
*   **A Premium User Experience:** From the fluid animations to the elegant "liquid glass" tab bar, every aspect of the UI has been meticulously crafted for a high-end, immersive feel.
*   **A Thriving Community:** WatchToHeal is more than a utility; it's a place for film lovers to connect, share, and discover together.
